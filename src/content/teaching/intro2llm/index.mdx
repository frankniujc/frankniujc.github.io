### Corrections
- L2 Slides Page 14: the example sentence should be "Every student takes a course."  
That sentence has two different interpretations:
    1. For every student, there exists a (potentially different) course that the student takes.  
    _Every student takes a course. Frank took three courses._
    2. There is a single course that every student takes.  
    _Every student takes a course. The course is Intro2LLM._
